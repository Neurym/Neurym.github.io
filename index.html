Our Mission

At Project ADA, we are committed to fostering inclusion and accessibility for individuals with disabilities. Our mission is to connect people with disabilities to opportunities and resources that empower them to lead independent and fulfilling lives.

Who We Are

Project ADA is a team of passionate individuals working to bridge the gap between accessibility and opportunity. We believe in creating a more inclusive world where everyone, regardless of their abilities, has access to the support and resources they need.
